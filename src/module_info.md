** The Fields of Normandy 2: A Solitaire Wargame

Vassal module for "The Fields of Normandy 2: A Solitaire Wargame" by Mike Lambo
https://boardgamegeek.com/boardgame/380356/the-fields-of-normandy-2-a-solitaire-wargame

This module has been released with the written permission of the author.

*** How to use the module:

When starting a new game, choose one of the available mission boards from the board picker.
The module includes Mission 1 through Mission 12.

Use the Counters toolbar button to open the counter palette.
The palette contains two counter sets:
- Standard Counters
- Alternate Counters

Both counter sets include the same main play aids and unit types:
- Generic markers: Cover, Mines, Smoke, Unknown, and Turn
- German units
- US units

The alternate counter set uses the alternate artwork credited below.

*** Main toolbar:

The toolbar includes several dice buttons:
- 1d6
- 2d6
- US Activation
- German Unit
- Fire

The US Activation, German Unit, and Fire buttons roll 2d6 and report the result in the chat log with colored dice icons.

The map toolbar also includes standard zoom controls, a save-map-as-image button, and a hide/show pieces button.

*** Unit activation:

Most unit counters can be marked as activated.
Right-click a unit and choose Activate/Deactivate, or use the unit's activation shortcut.
Activated units are shown with a cyan border.

To clear all activations, use either:
- The Reset Activations toolbar button
- The New Turn command on the Turn counter

Both commands reset activated units and remove their activation borders.
The New Turn command also removes Smoke markers from the map.

*** Turn marker:

Place the Turn counter from the counter palette on the map when you want an in-game turn reminder.
Right-click the Turn counter and choose New Turn to begin a new turn.
This resets unit activations and removes Smoke markers marked for new-turn cleanup.

*** US unit commands:

US units can be flipped between normal morale and low morale.
Right-click a US unit and choose Flip Morale.

US units also include a Move to Cover command.
This places the unit under a Cover marker.

In the alternate counter set, US units also include a Place Low Morale Marker command.

*** Smoke markers:

Smoke markers are temporary markers.
They are automatically removed when you use the New Turn command on the Turn counter.

*** German unit commands:

German units can be activated and reset like other units.
German units with facing can also be rotated clockwise or counter-clockwise from their right-click menu.

*** Notes:

This module is intended to support play of the physical game.
It does not enforce the full rules of The Fields of Normandy 2 automatically; players should follow the published rulebook.
